Welcome to Lovey Dovey, where you can find for yourself a soulmate (not lover but a pet) to stay by your side in the life journey. Our plan is to expand the shop around the world in 10 years to give the voluntary work a boost, especially areas which have the poor condition and performance in protecting pet's rights. At the moment, Asian countries such as China, Japan, India, Vietnam...
